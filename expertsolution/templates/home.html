Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?

GST, or Goods and Services Tax, is a unified indirect tax for the entire country, which replaced multiple cascading taxes previously levied by the central and state governments. It's a consumption-based tax levied on the supply of goods and services.

Who needs to register under GST?

Generally, businesses with an aggregate annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakh (for supply of goods) or ₹20 lakh (for supply of services) are required to register under GST. This threshold is ₹20 lakh (goods) and ₹10 lakh (services) for special category states in India.

What documents are required for GST registration?

Key documents include PAN card, Aadhaar card, proof of business registration, address proof for business premises, bank account details, and digital signature. The exact list can vary based on business type.

How can Expert Solution help my business with GST compliance?

We offer end-to-end GST services including registration, accurate monthly/quarterly return filing, expert tax consultancy to minimize liabilities, and comprehensive audit support, ensuring your business remains fully compliant.
